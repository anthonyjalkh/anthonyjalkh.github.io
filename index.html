Publicly Available Notes :

  • [FRENCH] Série de Fourier — Résumé d’une page (PDF)
    Résumé des séries de Fourier réelles, incluant les définitions des coefficients, les cas pairs/impairs et la formule de Parseval.
  • [FRENCH] Formes dérivées de la formule intégrale de Cauchy (PDF)
    Dérivation, de deux façons, des formes dérivées de la formule intégrale de Cauchy. La deuxième méthode fait preuve de bien plus de rigueur.
  • [FRENCH] Systèmes d'unités en physique (PDF)
    Récapitulation des diverses contraintes imposées par les principaux systèmes d'unités les plus omniprésents en physique nucléaire et en physique atomique.
  • The Power of the Variational Method (PDF)
    A motivation of the usefulness of the Variational Method in Quantum Mechanics followed by a concise, yet not fully rigorous derivation of it, demonstrating how it provides an upper bound to the ground-state energy of a quantum system. Note that this is not fully rigorous since I implicitly use the orthonormality of the eigenbasis of the Hamiltonian observable H to alleviate heavy notations in the derivation, especially when dealing with double summations. This ensures the paper is as streamlined as possible, allowing the reader to focus on the underlying mechanism of the Variational Method without being distracted by the algebraic manipulations that are necessary to deal with multiple summation indices.

About this website :

This website holds random samples of my personal notes and exploratory projects in physics and mathematics. It's not formal scientific research but a way for me to both gauge my learning progress over time, and have unrestricted access to very concise summaries of some of my older notes, largely from undergrad. More advanced unpublished or professional work is stored privately in my personal vaults. I exclusively upload here whenever I feel like it, as it mainly serves as a fun and engaging way to familiarize myself with the powerful Git+Overleaf workflow or even the newly released typesetting system, Typst. Therefore, the repository linked to this website serves as an experimentation tool that enables me to effectively enhance my actual professional work such as more advanced derivations, papers or reports that I store locally. Feel free to browse, and please don’t hesitate to contact me if you find any mistakes or have suggestions.
